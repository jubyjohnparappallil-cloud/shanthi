Our Story

Shanthi Wellness Medical Center

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Meera Krishnan, Shanthi Wellness was born from a deep conviction that true healing transcends the treatment of symptoms. Drawing from the 5,000-year-old science of Ayurveda, the gentle precision of Homeopathy, and the restorative power of Physiotherapy, we offer a sanctuary where ancient wisdom and modern clinical expertise converge.

Our philosophy is rooted in the understanding that every individual is unique — a distinct constellation of constitution, lifestyle, and life experience. We craft personalised healing journeys that address the root cause of imbalance, not merely its surface manifestations. Each consultation is an unhurried conversation; each treatment, a carefully considered ritual.

Over fifteen years, we have had the privilege of accompanying thousands of patients through transformations — from chronic pain to vitality, from anxiety to stillness, from illness to wholeness. Shanthi, meaning peace in Sanskrit, is not just our name. It is our promise.

Our Treatments

Classical Ayurvedic Therapies

Ancient healing therapies administered by expert practitioners for complete wellness.

Nasyam

Treatment done for certain types of paralysis, headaches, mental disorders etc., in which herbal juices and medicated oils are administered through the nostrils for a period of 7 to 14 days.

Book Now

Shirovasthi

In Shirovasthi, medicated oil is poured over a leather cap kept on the head. Very effective for headache, hair falling, burning sensation and treatments of other diseases of ear, nose and throat.

Book Now

Pizhichil

Lukewarm oil is poured in the body by squeezing cotton cloth soaked in oil. This is highly effective for Spondylosis, Hemiplegia, and Arthritis. It also helps in body and mind rejuvenation.

Book Now

Dhara

Continuous pouring of warm medicated liquids over the body or forehead. The rhythmic flow induces deep relaxation, calms the nervous system and is profoundly healing for stress-related conditions.

Book Now

Abhyanga

Full-body warm medicated oil massage that nourishes tissues, improves circulation, calms the nervous system and promotes deep relaxation. A cornerstone of Ayurvedic daily care.

Book Now

Panchakarma

The classical five-fold purification therapy — a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation programme. Removes deep-seated toxins and restores the body's natural balance and vitality.
