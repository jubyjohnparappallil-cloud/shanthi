Our Specialties

Healing Treatments

Each treatment is carefully tailored to your unique constitution and health goals, guided by our expert Ayurvedic physicians.

Pizhichil

Lukewarm oil is poured in the body by squeezing cotton cloth soaked in oil. Highly effective for Spondylosis, Hemiplegia, and Arthritis.

Dhara

Pouring of medicated oil done all over the body, beneficial for neuromuscular conditions, rheumatism and general debility.

Nasyam

Herbal juices and medicated oils administered through the nostrils for paralysis, headaches, and mental disorders.

Kizhi

Herbal poultice massage using warm bundles of medicinal herbs for joint pain, muscle stiffness, and neurological conditions.

Shirovasthi

Warm medicated oil retained on the head using a fitted cap — effective for neurological disorders and mental fatigue.

Abhyanga

Full-body warm oil massage using dosha-specific herbal oils, improving circulation and promoting deep relaxation.
