Our Story

Shanthi Wellness Medical Center

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Meera Krishnan, Shanthi Wellness was born from a deep conviction that true healing transcends the treatment of symptoms. Drawing from the 5,000-year-old science of Ayurveda, the gentle precision of Homeopathy, and the restorative power of Physiotherapy, we offer a sanctuary where ancient wisdom and modern clinical expertise converge.

Shanthi Wellness Medical Center is a unique facility that helps restore the natural balance of your mind, body and spirit by combining ancient medical techniques and complementary therapies. We provide authentic traditional Ayurveda treatments under the guidance of well-experienced Ayurveda physicians with years of international experience.

Our philosophy is rooted in the understanding that every individual is unique — a distinct constellation of constitution, lifestyle and life experience. We craft personalised healing journeys that address the root cause of imbalance, not merely its surface manifestations. Shanthi, meaning peace in Sanskrit, is not just our name. It is our promise.

Our Approach

What Makes Us Different

At Shanthi Wellness Medical Center, the evaluation begins by examining the patient through observation, touch and knowing how the person got to the current state of health. After identifying the root cause of the disorder, we move toward selecting the right Ayurveda treatment to battle the disease based on patient condition.

We look at the whole person — not only at the symptom and the disease the individual has been diagnosed with, but also the disease process, the root cause and constitution of the person. Thus keeping the person healthy and protecting against the further recurrence of the condition.

  • ✓ Personalised treatment plans based on your unique body constitution (Prakriti)
  • ✓ Integration of Ayurveda, Homeopathy and Physiotherapy under one roof
  • ✓ Root-cause resolution — not symptom suppression
  • ✓ Separate male and female fully equipped treatment rooms with bath facility
  • ✓ Highly qualified, experienced and licensed Panchakarma technicians

Our Journey

Milestones of Healing

From a small clinic with a single treatment room to a comprehensive wellness centre serving hundreds of patients — our journey has been guided by one constant: the commitment to authentic, compassionate healing.

2010

Founded

Dr. Meera Krishnan opens Shanthi Wellness with a single treatment room and a vision to bring authentic Ayurveda to Bengaluru.

2013

Homeopathy Wing Added

Dr. Hridya Pillai joins the team, establishing a dedicated Homeopathy clinic and expanding our integrative approach.

2016

Panchakarma Centre

A dedicated Panchakarma centre with separate male and female treatment suites is inaugurated, offering full classical detox programmes.
