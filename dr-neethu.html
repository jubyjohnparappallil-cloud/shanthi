🌿 About Dr. Neethu

Dr. Neethu B. Deepak is one among the finest Ayurvedic medical practitioners, having graduated in Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from Mahatma Gandhi University, Kerala — one of India's most prestigious institutions for Ayurvedic education.

She worked as a Junior Consultant at the National Research Institute for Panchakarma, Cheruthuruthy — a premier government institution dedicated to Panchakarma research and clinical practice. She subsequently served as Medical Officer at Nagarjuna Ayurvedic Centre, Kalady, one of Kerala's most respected Ayurvedic hospitals.

Ayurveda is not just a system of medicine — it is a way of life. True healing begins when we align our diet, exercise and daily routine with the wisdom of nature.

Dr. Neethu is known for her excellent patient care and is a strong believer in Ayurveda. She strictly recommends the Ayurvedic way of living — encompassing diet, exercise and medicine — in her medical practice. She is also an expert in Keraleeya Panchakarma treatments, the classical Kerala-specific therapeutic procedures that form the cornerstone of authentic Ayurvedic healing.

Keraleeya Panchakarma Treatments

💆 Panchakarma Expertise

As a specialist in Keraleeya Panchakarma, Dr. Neethu administers the full range of classical Kerala Ayurvedic therapies including Abhyanga, Shirodhara, Pizhichil, Njavarakizhi, Elakizhi, Nasyam, Shirovasthi and the complete Panchakarma detoxification programme.

Her training at the National Research Institute for Panchakarma — the country's foremost Panchakarma research centre — gives her a uniquely deep understanding of these therapies, both in their classical application and their evidence-based clinical use.

🌱 Her Philosophy

Dr. Neethu believes that the root cause of most modern diseases lies in the disconnect between our lifestyle and the natural rhythms described in Ayurvedic texts. Her approach goes beyond prescribing medicines — she guides each patient toward a sustainable Ayurvedic lifestyle that includes Dinacharya (daily routine), Ritucharya (seasonal regimen), appropriate diet and therapeutic yoga.

She is particularly passionate about women's health, having helped hundreds of patients manage PCOD, endometriosis and other gynaecological conditions through Ayurvedic protocols — without the side effects of hormonal medications.
