Ayurveda is a 5,000-year-old holistic healing system from India. It works on the principle that every individual has a unique body constitution (Prakriti) determined by three vital energies called doshas — Vata, Pitta and Kapha. When these doshas are in balance, we are healthy. When they are imbalanced, disease occurs. Ayurvedic treatment aims to restore this balance through personalised diet, lifestyle, herbal medicines and therapeutic treatments.
Your first consultation is an unhurried conversation with our Ayurvedic physician. The doctor will assess your body constitution (Prakriti) through pulse diagnosis (Nadi Pariksha), observation and detailed questioning about your health history, lifestyle, diet and current symptoms. Based on this assessment, a personalised treatment plan will be recommended. The consultation typically takes 45–60 minutes.
Panchakarma is the classical five-fold purification therapy of Ayurveda. It involves five procedures — Vamana (emesis), Virechana (purgation), Anuvasana (oil enema), Niruha (decoction enema) and Nasya (nasal administration). It is suitable for people with chronic diseases, those seeking deep detoxification, or anyone wanting to rejuvenate their body and mind. A prior consultation is essential to determine which procedures are appropriate for you.
Treatment duration varies depending on the condition and the therapy prescribed. Single sessions like Abhyanga or Shirodhara take 45–90 minutes. Treatment programmes typically range from 7 to 21 days for conditions like arthritis, spondylosis or neurological disorders. Panchakarma programmes are usually 14–21 days. Your doctor will recommend the appropriate duration during your consultation.
When administered by qualified practitioners using authentic herbal medicines and oils, Ayurvedic treatments are generally very safe. At Shanthi Wellness, all treatments are prescribed and supervised by qualified Ayurvedic physicians. Herbal medicines are prepared according to classical formulations. Some treatments may cause mild detox reactions (fatigue, loose stools) which are normal and temporary. We always conduct a thorough assessment before recommending any treatment.

Ayurveda is effective for a wide range of conditions including arthritis, spondylosis, back pain, migraine, sinusitis, skin disorders, digestive problems, hormonal imbalances, stress, anxiety, insomnia, obesity, neurological conditions, and many chronic diseases. It is also highly effective as a preventive and rejuvenative therapy for healthy individuals seeking to maintain optimal health and vitality.
Shirodhara involves a continuous stream of warm medicated oil poured over the forehead in a rhythmic flow for 30–45 minutes. It is profoundly calming for the nervous system and is highly effective for stress, anxiety, insomnia, migraine, hypertension, depression, hair loss, premature greying, and neurological conditions. Many patients describe it as one of the most deeply relaxing experiences of their lives.
