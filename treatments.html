AYURVEDA offers a range of traditional therapies and herbal medicines to balance the bodily principles and to integrate body, mind and spirit. These therapies can be used both to cure various ailments as well as to improve general health (Rejuvenation). The effect of therapies differs with the oil and other ingredients used. Having identified the body constitution (Prakruti) and type of ailment (Vikruti), a suitable type of therapy is carefully selected.
Ayurveda

Abhyanga

A traditional full-body massage done with medicated herbal oils by experienced therapists in a synchronized manner for 45 minutes. Treats the whole body physically, mentally and emotionally while balancing the doshas. Relieves joints and muscles from stiffness, increases blood circulation and encourages quick removal of metabolic wastes.

⏱ 45 minutes  |  Daily or as prescribed

Kerala Specialty

Pizhichil (Thailadhara)

A king among traditional therapies — a beautiful combination of Snehana (oleation) and Swedana (sudation). The whole body is subjected to streams of lukewarm medicated oil with simultaneous soft massage. More than three litres of medicated oil is used. Very useful for rheumatic diseases, arthritis, paralysis, sexual weakness and neurological disorders.

⏱ 60–90 minutes  |  7–14 days

Book Now →
Kerala Specialty

Njavarakizhi

A massage with small cotton cloth boluses filled with cooked Njavara rice after a liberal application of medicated oil. The rice is cooked in cow's milk and herbal decoction. Highly rejuvenating and nourishing — strengthens the nervous system and improves the overall appearance of the skin. Effective for neurological disorders, rheumatism and arthritis.

⏱ 60–90 minutes  |  7–14 days

Ayurveda

Shirodhara

A continuous stream of warm medicated oil poured over the forehead in a rhythmic flow. Profoundly calming for the nervous system and mind. Highly effective for stress, anxiety, insomnia, migraine, hypertension and neurological conditions. One of the most iconic and deeply healing of all Ayurvedic therapies.

⏱ 45–60 minutes  |  7–21 days

Ayurveda

Shirovasthi

Medicated oil is poured over a leather cap kept on the head and retained for a prescribed period. Very effective for headache, hair falling, burning sensation and treatments of other diseases of ear, nose and throat. Deeply nourishes the nervous system and brain tissues.

⏱ 45–60 minutes  |  7 days

Kerala Specialty

Elakizhi (Pathrapotala Sweda)

Begins with the application of suitable medicated oil to the entire body. The body is then subjected to profuse sweating through a massage with boluses of fresh leaves and herbal powders. Highly effective for diseases of the musculoskeletal system, arthritis, spondylosis and sports injuries.

⏱ 45–60 minutes  |  7–14 days

Ayurveda

Nasyam

Treatment done for certain types of paralysis, headaches, mental disorders etc., in which herbal juices and medicated oils are administered through the nostrils. Highly effective for sinusitis, migraine, cervical spondylosis and neurological conditions of the head and neck region.

⏱ 30–45 minutes  |  7–14 days

Detox Programme

Panchakarma

The classical five-fold purification therapy — a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation programme. Removes deep-seated toxins and restores the body's natural balance and vitality. The five procedures are Vamana, Virechana, Anuvasana, Niruha and Nasya. Effective even for otherwise incurable diseases.
